ABOUT SEJONG205

Company Overview

SEJONG205 is a multi-vendor platform dedicated to Korean products in Poland, connecting trusted local vendors with customers who love K-culture.

Company Overview Vision & Mission Management & Team History & Milestones

SEJONG205 at a Glance

SEJONG205 is the first Korean-products-only multi-vendor online platform in Poland. Headquartered in Warsaw, we connect verified local vendors with customers who seek authentic K-Food, K-Beauty, K-Lifestyle and K-Pop products.

Our role is simple: we create a reliable, curated marketplace so that vendors can focus on great products, and customers can enjoy Korean culture with confidence.

Platform Type

A dedicated marketplace for Korean products, designed for both everyday customers and K-culture enthusiasts in Poland.

Business Model

Commission-only structure (8–12%) with no entry fees, plus optional managed logistics services for selected vendors.

Vendor Network

Local Polish vendors supply products under clear guidelines on pricing, quality, delivery and customer support.

Customer Promise

Authentic products, transparent pricing, and a consistent shopping experience powered by reliable logistics partners.

Our Core Values

Business Structure in Brief

For Vendors

  • No onboarding fee, only sales commission.
  • Support for pricing guidelines and product data templates.
  • Two delivery options: direct shipping or SEJONG205-managed logistics.

For Customers

  • Single platform for Korean food, beauty, lifestyle and K-Pop items.
  • Standardized policies for delivery, returns and customer service.
  • Carefully selected products with clear descriptions and origin.
